Company Profile

Bullseye Dosing Technology Co., Ltd

Bullseye is dedicated to providing customized, high-precision, and fast weight-fill technology solutions for companies in diverse industrial sectors. Our R&D team is committed to developing specialized machinery tailored to your specific needs. As a leading provider of high-precision dispensing and weighing technology solutions in China, we excel in advanced powder and liquid automatic batching technologies, bridging the gap in the field of high-precision ingredient mixing.

Our expertise lies in the realm of high-precision filling, where we design and develop state-of-the-art batching machines and integrated software systems. These are adept at handling a broad spectrum of flowable materials, including powders, granules, liquids, and colloids. Our innovative solutions are designed to serve a multitude of industries, with a focus on optimizing processes and production workflows that demand the utmost precision in filling.

With a global customer base, we are committed to fulfilling each of your requirements promptly, ensuring exceptional service and unparalleled quality. At Bullseye, we are not just about meeting expectations; we aim to exceed them, offering reliable and efficient solutions worldwide.
